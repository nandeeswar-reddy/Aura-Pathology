Analytics Platform

Aura is an enterprise-grade AI system engineered for precision histopathology. Powered by a Hybrid CNN (ResNet-34) and Vision Transformer (ViT-Tiny) architecture, the platform features Adaptive Attention Mechanisms and GradCAM explainability. Designed to process complex tissue morphologies, Aura provides sub-second diagnostic inferences across 5 distinct tumor pathologies, delivering unprecedented clinical validation and transparency for state-of-the-art oncology research.

Validation & Research Highlights

Evaluating the hybrid architecture against clinical datasets.

Multi-Class Diagnostic Accuracy

The combination of ResNet convolutional features (spatial) and Vision Transformer sequences (global context) allows for reliable separation of lung squamous cell carcinoma from lung adenocarcinoma and benign lung tissue.

Colon Adenocarcinoma Accuracy99.72%
Lung Adenocarcinoma Accuracy99.55%
Lung Squamous Cell Carcinoma Accuracy99.61%

Explainability Paradigm (Grad-CAM)

Medical researchers require trust. Rather than outputting classification scores blindly, AURA visualizes active feature grids in the final convolutional layers. This maps model attention directly back to cellular structures (nuclear atypia, stroma spacing) of interest to pathologists.
